# Legal and Ethical Use

This document outlines the legal and ethical principles that apply to the use of this repository.

The purpose of this repository is education and understanding, not circumvention or misuse.

---

## Educational purpose

All materials provided in this repository are intended for educational and research purposes only.  
They are designed to help learners understand how microcontrollers, firmware, and embedded systems work.

This repository is not intended to replace professional tools or commercial products.

---

## Use of software and firmware

No proprietary firmware, protected software, or confidential material is distributed as part of this repository.

Users are expected to:
- work with their own code, or  
- use software and firmware that are openly available and legally obtained  

Redistribution of third-party proprietary firmware is neither supported nor encouraged.

---

## Reverse engineering activities

Some advanced topics may involve the study of compiled firmware.

These activities are:
- optional  
- educational in nature  
- focused on understanding structure and behaviour  

They are **not** intended to:
- bypass security mechanisms  
- remove protections  
- violate software licenses  

Users are responsible for ensuring compliance with local laws and applicable licenses.

---

## Ethical considerations

Users of this repository are expected to act responsibly and ethically.

This includes:
- respecting intellectual property  
- respecting licenses and terms of use  
- using the material for learning, teaching, or research  

When in doubt, users should seek guidance from an educator or legal advisor.

---

## Responsibility

The authors of this repository do not encourage or support illegal activity.

Responsibility for how the materials are used lies entirely with the user.

---

This document exists to support responsible learning and educational use worldwide.
